# **Manuscript: OdinPad's Drafting Studio**

---

## **1. Vision & Purpose**

**Manuscript** is OdinPad's dedicated drafting environment. The space where structured plans from Canvas become living prose, and where finished scenes flow together into a complete manuscript. If the Idea Web captures sparks, the Sandbox expands them, and Canvas assembles them into structure, Manuscript is where the story finally breathes on the page.

Manuscript provides a distraction-free writing environment that seamlessly integrates with the rest of OdinPad. Writers can draft in focused solitude while maintaining instant access to their worldbuilding, character profiles, and structural plans. The editor supports both linear and non-linear workflows, allowing writers to follow inspiration wherever it leads while keeping everything organized and connected.

Manuscript is designed to feel like slipping into the story itself: clean, warm, focused, and deeply connected to everything else in OdinPad. Writers can draft linearly or jump non-linearly, reference worldbuilding without breaking flow, track progress visually, protect versions safely, and revise with confidence—all while staying immersed in the cozy writer's-den aesthetic (soft parchment backgrounds, fountain-pen cursor options, ambient rain or fireplace sounds).

**Core Philosophy:** Writing should feel like writing—not like managing files. Manuscript gets out of your way when you need flow and gives you power when you need precision. Make drafting feel effortless and joyful. Eliminate friction between planning and writing. Keep the entire novel ecosystem (structure, codex, notes, versions) one glance or click away—without ever leaving the page.

## **4. The Manuscript Components**

Manuscript consists of seven interconnected systems, each designed to support a different aspect of the drafting and revision process:

1. **The Editor** — Core writing environment with rich text support
2. **Focus Mode** — Distraction-free full-screen writing with deep personalization
3. **Non-Linear Workflow** — Write anywhere, reorganize anytime
4. **Worldbuilding Integration** — Seamless access to Story Wiki and Sandbox via @mentions
5. **Meta-Panel** — Scene-level intelligence drawer for metadata, tracking, and continuity
6. **Revision & Version History** — Complete control over your writing journey with snapshots
7. **Preview & Export** — See your work as readers will see it

## **5. The Editor**

### **Overview**

The Editor is the heart of Manuscript—a clean, modern writing environment that prioritizes flow while providing powerful features exactly when you need them.

**Core Concept:** The best writing tool is the one you don't notice while using.

### **Features**

### **Rich Text Formatting**

- **Standard Formatting:** Bold, italic, underline, strikethrough
- **Headings:** H1, H2, H3 for section organization within scenes
- **Lists:** Bulleted and numbered lists with proper indentation
- **Block Quotes:** For epigraphs, internal dialogue, or formatting
- **Horizontal Rules:** Scene breaks and section dividers
- **Superscript/Subscript:** For footnotes, annotations, or worldbuilding asides
- **Inline Images:** Drag from Story Wiki mood boards or local upload with caption/resizing
- **Alignment:** Left/center text alignment and indent controls

### **Markdown Support**

- **Seamless Toggle:** Switch between Rich Text and Markdown views
- **Live Preview:** See formatted text while writing in Markdown
- **Keyboard Shortcuts:** Familiar Markdown syntax (e.g., `#` for headings, `*` for bold)
- **Dual Experience:** Use whichever feels natural; switch at any time

### **Writing Aids**

- **Spellcheck:** Browser-integrated spellcheck with custom dictionary support
- **Thesaurus:** Built-in thesaurus for finding the perfect word (optional panel) — double-click any word for synonym suggestions
- **Grammar Suggestions:** Integration with third-party tools like ProWritingAid, Grammarly
- **Word Count:** Live word count with goals and targets; progress ring with motivational nudges
- **Readability Score:** Optional insights into sentence complexity

### **Auto-Save & Backup**

- **Continuous Saving:** Every keystroke saved automatically (every 3 seconds)
- **Cloud Sync:** Real-time sync across devices via Supabase
- **Offline Support:** PWA with IndexedDB caching; sync when reconnected
- **Backup History:** All changes preserved in revision history

### **Search & Quick Creation**

- **Global Search:** Spotlight-style search (Cmd/Ctrl + K) to find articles, scenes, or codex items instantly
- **Quick Article Creation Button:** Add new elements (e.g., characters, locations) during drafting without leaving the editor — modal form appears, basic details added, automatically linked to current scene
- **Idea Web Integration:** Search and quick-create features extend to Idea Web database, enabling seamless note integration

### **Use Case: The Flow State Writer**

**Scenario:** Ama is in the zone, words pouring onto the page. She doesn't want to think about saving, formatting, or anything but the story.

**Editor Experience:**

- Opens scene, starts typing immediately
- Auto-save works silently in background
- Markdown handles formatting without breaking flow
- Spellcheck underlines errors but doesn't interrupt
- Word count updates in sidebar for progress awareness

**Benefits:** Flow remains unbroken. Technical concerns handled automatically.

## **6. Focus Mode**

### **Overview**

Focus Mode transforms Manuscript into a distraction-free writing sanctuary—full-screen, clean, and deeply customizable. It's designed for deep writing sessions where the outside world fades away and only the story remains. Expanded customization turns it from a simple fullscreen toggle into a deeply personal ritual space.

**Core Concept:** When you're writing, nothing else should exist.

### **Features**

### **Full-Screen Immersion**

- **Full-Screen Mode:** Entire screen dedicated to writing
- **Hide All UI:** Menus, toolbars, sidebars disappear
- **Fade on Inactivity:** Controls fade after seconds of typing; reappear on hover

### **Typewriter Mode**

- **Cursor Centered:** Active line always in the middle of the screen
- **Smooth Scrolling:** Text scrolls as you type, keeping cursor centered
- **Reduced Eye Movement:** Less eye strain during long sessions
- **Typewriter Scroll Behavior Options:**
  - **Classic:** Cursor always centered
  - **Gentle:** Scrolls only when cursor nears bottom third
  - **Page-Turn:** Smooth vertical wipe on paragraph end

### **Customizable Themes**

| **Theme**            | **Description**                           | **Unlock Method**   |
| -------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | ------------------- |
| **Classic Light**    | Clean white background, dark text         | Default             |
| **Dark Mode**        | Easy on eyes for night writing            | Default             |
| **Sepia**            | Warm, paper-like background               | Default             |
| **Parchment**        | Cozy, textured background                 | Default             |
| **Enchanted Forest** | Deep green with soft leaf shadows         | Gamification unlock |
| **Midnight Library** | Dark wood shelves + faint book glow       | Gamification unlock |
| **Seaside Cabin**    | Soft blue-gray with rain streaks          | Gamification unlock |
| **Custom Upload**    | User's own cozy photo (desk, window view) | Phase 2             |

### **Visual Atmosphere Customizations**

- **Vignette & Lighting:** Adjustable soft fade at edges (intensity 0–100%), warm/cool color tint, subtle page glow around text
- **Page Textures:** Aged paper, vellum, linen options
- **Cursor Styles:** Classic quill, modern fountain pen, glowing ink drop

### **Customizable Typography**

- **Font Families:** Serif (Crimson Text, EB Garamond, Georgia), Sans-Serif (Inter, Lora, Open Sans), Typewriter (Courier Prime)
- **Font Size:** 14–24 pt adjustable
- **Line Height:** 1.4–2.0 spacing (saved per project or global)
- **Margins:** Adjustable page margins (narrow for focus, wide for "book page" feel)

### **Ambient Soundscapes**

- **Optional Soundscapes:** Rain on window, fireplace crackle, forest night (owls, soft wind), coffee shop hum, vintage typewriter clack (syncs with keystrokes), ocean waves
- **Keystroke Sounds:** Optional soft mechanical click or pen scratch
- **White/Pink/Brown Noise:** Overlay options for focus
- **Volume Control:** Adjustable intensity per sound
- **Loop Seamlessly:** No breaks in audio flow

### **Session Tools & Rituals**

- **Session Timer:** Pomodoro-style (25/5, 50/10, custom), gentle chime + visual wind-down
- **Word Goal Overlay:** Subtle progress ring in corner ("320 / 1,000 words")
- **Streak Protection Reminder:** If low-energy day: "One sentence still protects your streak"
- **Quick Exit Shortcut:** Esc or Cmd/Ctrl + . to minimize without breaking immersion
- **Custom Rituals:** Save favorite Focus setups as "Rituals" (e.g., "Rainy Evening Drafting" = forest theme + rain sound + typewriter scroll)
- **Personal Goals:** User-defined Focus targets ("3 hours/week") with progress widgets

### **Split-Screen Companions (Non-Distracting Reference)**

- **Canvas Sidebar:** Show current scene's Canvas card (synopsis, linked Wiki entities, timeline position)
- **Meta-Panel Peek:** Collapsible bottom drawer with key scene metadata
- **Story Wiki Quick-Lookup:** Floating search that appears on @-mention hover (non-intrusive popover)
- **Snapshot Quick-Access:** Mini-timeline of last 3 snapshots in corner (one-click revert)

### **Gamification Integration**

- **Focus Streak:** Consecutive days in Focus Mode (protected by 1-sentence rule)
- **Deep Work Badges:** "Flow State" (1 hr uninterrupted), "Marathon Writer" (3+ hrs), "Midnight Muse" (session after 10 PM)
- **Session Goals:** Word count targets tie into Writer's Odyssey streaks

### **Use Case: The Deep Work Session**

**Scenario:** Kofi has two hours to write before his day job. He needs maximum focus.

**Focus Mode Workflow:**

1. Opens Focus Mode with Typewriter enabled
2. Selects Dark Mode theme and sets 30-minute timer
3. Adds soft rain ambient sound
4. Opens split-screen with scene synopsis from Canvas
5. Writes without interruption until timer chimes
6. Takes a break, repeats for three sessions

**Benefits:** Maximum productivity. Flow state achieved. Distractions eliminated.

## **7. Non-Linear Workflow**

### **Overview**

Stories rarely come in order. Inspiration strikes for the climax while you're writing Chapter 3. A character's backstory emerges in the middle of drafting. Manuscript embraces non-linear workflows, allowing writers to jump anywhere without losing their place or organizational sanity.

**Core Concept:** Write what calls to you. Structure can always be rearranged.

### **Features**

### **Jump Anywhere, Anytime**

- **Scene Navigation:** Binder shows all scenes; click any to open
- **Quick Search:** Spotlight-style search (Cmd/Ctrl + K) to jump to any scene, chapter, or codex entry
- **Recent Scenes:** Quick list of recently edited scenes
- **Bookmarks:** Mark scenes you're actively working on for quick access
- **Keyboard Shortcuts:** Cmd/Ctrl + [1–9] for last 9 scenes

### **Write Out of Order**

- **No Constraints:** Write Chapter 17 before Chapter 3
- **Placeholder Scenes:** Create skeleton scenes to fill later
- **Notes as Placeholders:** Draft notes can become scenes when ready
- **Unlinked Scenes:** Write scenes without assigning to chapters; organize later

### **Seamless Reorganization**

- **Drag-and-Drop:** Move scenes between chapters in Binder
- **Bulk Operations:** Select multiple scenes to move together
- **Auto-Updating:** All links, references, and metadata update automatically
- **Undo Support:** Accidental moves can be undone

### **Scrivenings-Style Continuous View ("Stitch Mode")**

- **Continuous Scroll:** Multiple scenes displayed as one uninterrupted document
- **Seamless Navigation:** Flow from scene to scene without interruption
- **Scene Breaks:** Visual markers (# or subtle line) indicate where scenes begin and end (collapsible)
- **Edit Anywhere:** Make changes across scenes without switching files
- **Ideal For:** Linear read-throughs, polishing flow, or writing long connected sequences

### **Placeholder System**

- **TODO Markers:** `TODO:` in text creates visual reminders
- **Highlighted Sections:** Mark sections that need expansion or revision
- **Comment Notes:** Add comments to yourself that don't appear in final draft
- **Bracket Drafting:** `[Write chase scene here]` style placeholders

### **Use Case: The Non-Linear Drafter**

**Scenario:** Yaw wakes up with the perfect ending in his head, but he's only on Chapter 4.

**Non-Linear Workflow:**

1. Opens Manuscript, navigates to Chapter 20 (placeholder scene)
2. Writes the ending while inspiration is fresh
3. Adds TODO marker: `[Set up this reveal in Chapter 7]`
4. Returns to Chapter 4, continues where he left off
5. Later, when he reaches Chapter 7, search for TODO reminds him to add foreshadowing

**Benefits:** Inspiration captured immediately. No wasted momentum. Structure adapts to creativity.

## **9. Meta-Panel: Scene & Chapter Intelligence Drawer**

### **Overview**

The Meta-Panel is a collapsible, always-available intelligence drawer attached to every scene and chapter in the Manuscript editor. It acts as the "brain" of each writing unit—storing structured context, tracking key story elements, providing quick-reference data, and enabling precise continuity and progress management without ever leaving the prose flow.

Think of it as a living index card + dashboard hybrid pinned right beside (or below) your text: rich enough for serious plotters to track complex threads, lightweight enough for pantsers to glance at when needed, and smart enough to surface relevant Story Wiki data, Canvas metadata, and AI editorial insights at the perfect moment.

**Core Design Principles:**

- **Zero Friction Access:** Toggle with one click or keyboard shortcut (Cmd/Ctrl + M); auto-collapses when typing resumes
- **Cozy & Glanceable:** Clean, warm layout (soft cards, subtle icons, parchment accents) optimized for quick scans during flow state
- **Fully Bidirectional:** Changes here instantly update Canvas cards, Binder metadata, Timeline events, and Story Wiki references (and vice versa)
- **AI as Silent Assistant:** Auto-fills, detects gaps, suggests links/flags—never writes content

### **Features**

### **Core Fields (Always Visible – Top Section)**

| **Field**                 | **Description**                                                                      | **Integration**                                          |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Scene/Chapter Title**   | Editable inline                                                                      | Syncs with Binder/Canvas card title                      |
| **Synopsis**              | Short (150–300 word) summary; rich-text enabled for quick bullet points or key beats | Auto-suggest fill from Canvas card synopsis              |
| **POV Character(s)**      | Multi-select dropdown linked to Story Wiki                                           | Visual avatar badge + quick link to full Wiki profile    |
| **Primary Location**      | Dropdown from Story Wiki locations                                                   | Map pin thumbnail if attached; secondary locations field |
| **Emotional Tone / Mood** | Color-coded tag selector (joy, tension, despair, reflective, triumphant, etc.)       | Feeds Canvas emotional heat map and pacing analysis      |
| **Status**                | Progress flag: Outline, To Do, First Draft, In Revision, Polished, Complete          | Color-coded dot + progress percentage                    |
| **Word Count**            | Live counter + target goal                                                           | Progress ring + motivational nudge                       |

### **Story Element Tracking (Linked & Auto-Updating)**

- **Key Characters Present:** Multi-select from Story Wiki → auto-populates from @-mentions in text or Canvas card links; shows presence count across project
- **Key Locations Present:** Same auto-linking behavior; displays map thumbnails inline
- **Plot Threads / Subplots:** Tag selector for active threads (Main Plot, Romance A, Mystery B, Redemption Arc); visual thread color swatch; tracks thread balance
- **Story Arcs Impacted:** Dropdown showing which character arcs or major arcs this scene advances; pulls from Canvas arc definitions; updates arc progress bars
- **Timeline Position:** Inherited from Canvas Timeline → shows approximate in-universe date/time + chapter/scene sequence number; click to jump to Timeline view

### **Planning & Continuity Aids**

- **Setup / Payoff Notes:** Dedicated field for writer to flag elements introduced here that need later payoff; AI editorial scans and reminds in later scenes
- **Foreshadowing Checklist:** Bullet list of planted seeds (auto-detected from @-mentions or manual tags); cross-references future scenes where payoff occurs
- **Continuity Flags:** AI-generated warnings (red/yellow badges):
  - "POV shift without transition scene?"
  - "Character age mismatch detected?"
  - "Magic rule violation flagged in Story Wiki?"
  - Dismiss or snooze per flag; improves with user feedback

### **Revision & Progress Tracking**

- **Revision Notes:** Threaded comment-style field for self-notes or future beta feedback ("Tighten dialogue here", "Add sensory detail"); collapsible thread view; supports @-mentions to self for reminders
- **Snapshot History Quick-Access:** Mini-timeline of last 5 snapshots for this scene/chapter; one-click revert or compare diff without opening full history panel
- **Scene Goal / Intent:** One-line field: "Advance romance subplot + plant clue for mystery"; helps during revision: "Did this scene achieve its intent?" checkbox

### **Visual & Habit Enhancers**

- **Mood-Board Preview:** Small gallery of pinned images from Canvas/Story Wiki (color palettes, character art, setting refs); hover to enlarge; drag to insert inline image into text
- **Gamification Micro-Stats:** Tiny progress indicators: daily word streak counter, scene completion badge (earned when status = Complete), "Focus Streak: 3 days" nudge

### **Integration Depth**

- **Canvas:** Meta-Panel fields mirror Canvas card metadata; drag new cards from Canvas → auto-populates meta
- **Story Wiki:** All character/location/arc links are live; editing Wiki auto-updates presence lists and flags
- **Sandbox:** Pull unfinished Sandbox outputs directly into meta-notes or intent field
- **Gamification System:** Completing meta-fields (e.g., filling POV + tone + threads) earns micro-points; "Meta Master" badge for fully populated scenes

### **Use Case: The Multi-POV Tracker**

**Scenario:** Ama's novel has 5 POV characters. She needs to ensure each appears regularly.

**Meta-Panel Workflow:**

1. Assigns POV character to every scene via Meta-Panel
2. Opens Architect's Desk table view
3. Sorts by POV column to see distribution
4. Spots that one character hasn't appeared in 8 chapters
5. Adds a scene to maintain balance
6. Filters to show only that POV to check development

**Benefits:** Complete visibility. Easy tracking. Balanced narratives.

## **10. Revision & Version History: Snapshot History UX**

### **Overview**

The Snapshot History is OdinPad's gentle, confidence-building safety net for every scene and chapter in the Manuscript. It turns the natural fear of "what if I ruin this?" into quiet reassurance: every meaningful change is automatically preserved, visually comparable, and effortlessly restorable—without ever cluttering the workspace or breaking creative flow.

The UX is designed to feel like flipping through a well-loved writer's journal: warm, chronological, glanceable, and forgiving. Writers should never feel lost in version hell; instead, they should feel quietly protected, able to experiment boldly, and always one click away from any past state of their prose.

**Core Design Principles:**

- **Invisible Until Needed:** Snapshots work silently in the background; the writer only sees them when they want to look
- **Cozy & Human-Scale:** Soft parchment timeline aesthetic, gentle animations, friendly language ("Last good version from Tuesday evening")
- **Zero Cognitive Load:** No complex menus; most actions are one or two clicks
- **Emotional Safety:** Reassuring micro-feedback ("Your last 3 hours are safe") and panic buttons ("Revert to Last Good")
- **Context-Rich:** Every snapshot carries enough metadata to jog memory without opening the full text

### **Features**

### **Background Capture – Invisible & Automatic**

- **Frequency:** Auto-snapshot every 3 minutes (configurable 1–10 min), on every major edit (100+ words added/deleted, scene reorder, metadata change), and on Focus Mode exit
- **Manual Snapshots:** One-click "Save Version" button (top-right corner of editor or Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + S); optional 1-line label prompt ("Before major rewrite")
- **Smart Thinning:** Older snapshots auto-compress (keep full text for last 7 days, diffs only beyond that) to prevent storage bloat

### **Access Points – Frictionless Entry**

- **Primary Trigger:** Small, subtle clock icon in top-right editor header (glows faintly when new snapshot created); hover tooltip: "Last snapshot: 2 min ago – 347 words added"; click → opens Snapshot Drawer
- **Secondary Triggers:**
  - Right-click in text → "View History…"
  - Keyboard shortcut: Cmd/Ctrl + H → instant drawer
  - Meta-Panel → "Snapshot History" quick-link (shows mini-timeline of last 5)
  - After risky action (bulk paste, major delete) → proactive popover: "Big change detected—want to save a snapshot first?"

### **Snapshot Drawer – The Visual Heart**

- **Layout:** Vertical timeline scroll (newest at top)
- **Each Entry Contains:**
  - Date + relative time ("Today, 3:42 PM – 8 min ago")
  - Change summary ("+1,240 words, 3 deletions, metadata updated")
  - Optional user label (bold if present, e.g., "Before killing off secondary character")
  - Tiny preview snippet (first 100–150 characters of text)
  - Word-count delta badge (+/- compared to current)
  - Status dot (green = manual, gray = auto)
- **Visual Warmth:** Soft parchment background, gentle drop-shadow cards, faint ink-stain dividers between days
- **Collapsible Days:** Group by date; expand only today's or yesterday's by default

### **Interaction Patterns – Fast & Forgiving**

- **Quick Revert:** Hover any snapshot → ghost button "Revert to This" (one-click full scene restore); confirmation popover: "Revert to version from 2:15 PM? Current changes will be saved as new snapshot."
- **Side-by-Side Compare:** Click any snapshot → splits editor into left/right diff view; red = deleted, green = added, subtle gray = unchanged; highlight navigation: jump to next change with arrow buttons; cherry-pick: select paragraphs → "Copy to Current" button
- **Panic Button – "Revert to Last Good":** Top-right emergency button (only visible after 5+ minutes of editing); one-click reverts to most recent manual snapshot or last stable auto-snapshot; toast: "Reverted safely. Your latest changes are saved as 'Emergency Backup'."

### **Search & Filter**

- Tiny search bar at top of drawer: filter by keyword in snapshot notes or text preview
- Filters: Manual only, Today/Yesterday/This Week, Word-count change >500, Labeled only

### **Meta-Panel Integration – Instant Context**

- Mini-timeline of last 5 snapshots always visible in Meta-Panel (horizontal scroll)
- Hover snapshot → tooltip shows change summary + preview
- Click → opens full drawer pre-scrolled to that version
- "Compare from Meta" button: instantly diffs current text against any listed snapshot

### **Gamification & Emotional Rewards**

- **Micro-Badges:** "Time Traveler" (use revert 5 times), "Safety Net" (create 10 manual snapshots), "Bold Experimenter" (revert after 1,000+ word change)
- **Reassuring Nudges:** After revert → "You're safe. Keep going." toast + confetti animation (subtle, optional toggle)
- **Insights:** Weekly report—"You saved 47 snapshots this week—your work is well protected!"

### **Integration Depth**

- **Canvas:** Snapshot revert updates linked Canvas card word-count/progress bar
- **Story Wiki:** If revert affects @-mentioned entities, prompts: "Character details changed—review Wiki links?"
- **Sandbox:** Reverted scenes can be dragged back to Sandbox as "alternate branch" for further exploration
- **Gamification System:** Snapshot usage counts toward "Safe Writer" streak protection

### **Use Case: The Experimenting Reviser**

**Scenario:** Kwame wants to try a different opening for his novel but doesn't want to lose his current version.

**Version History Workflow:**

1. Takes manual snapshot: "Original Opening - Pre-Experiment"
2. Rewrites opening scene extensively
3. Decides original was better after all
4. Opens Revision History, selects "Original Opening - Pre-Experiment"
5. Compares side-by-side to see what he'd lose
6. Restores original with one click

**Benefits:** Fearless experimentation. No permanent changes without review. Complete control.

## **11. Manuscript Preview**

### **Overview**

Manuscript Preview allows writers to see their work as readers will experience it—formatted as a book, with proper typography, chapter breaks, and layout. This reading mode is essential for proofreading, flow checking, and export preparation.

## **12. AI Editorial: The Silent Structural & Consistency Assistant**

### **Core Philosophy & Guardrails**

OdinPad's AI is strictly editorial—a quiet, behind-the-scenes partner that illuminates problems, reveals patterns, suggests improvements, and protects continuity without ever taking creative control.

| **Principle**                  | **Implementation**                                                                                     |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **0% Generative Text**         | AI never writes, rewrites, describes, expands, or completes prose                                      |
| **User Approval Required**     | Every suggestion (link, flag, placement) must be explicitly accepted                                   |
| **Opt-In Depth**               | Users choose how much assistance they want (minimal → full structural intelligence)                    |
| **Local-First Where Possible** | Most detection and simple suggestions run client-side or on edge functions                             |
| **Ethical & Transparent**      | Every insight includes a brief explanation ("Why flagged?"); feedback loop improves accuracy over time |
| **Privacy-First**              | No training on user content; analysis is project-isolated                                              |

### **Global AI Capabilities (Available in Every Module)**

### **Entity Recognition & Auto-Linking**

- Detects names of characters, locations, items, factions, magic systems as you type
- Suggests inline @-links to existing Story Wiki entries (popover preview on hover)
- Flags inconsistencies ("'Elara' spelled three ways—standardize?")
- Auto-populates Meta-Panel "Key Characters/Locations Present" lists
- **Impact:** Eliminates manual searching; catches continuity errors early

### **Consistency & Contradiction Guardian**

- Scans text + metadata for contradictions against Story Wiki rules (magic costs, character traits, timeline events, relationship states)
- Real-time sidebar badges (yellow = warning, red = hard conflict)
- **Example Alerts:**
  - "Fire spell used here, but Wiki rule forbids fire magic."
  - "Kofi is 17 in this scene but 19 two chapters earlier."
  - "Elara fears heights but just climbed a tower—review motivation?"
- Suggests resolution paths (non-prescriptive): "Link to Wiki rule?" "Add contradiction note in Meta-Panel?"

### **Pattern & Motif Highlighter**

- Detects recurring tags, keywords, emotional tones, or motifs across scenes/ideas
- Visual overlays: color threads connecting related cards (e.g., red betrayal arc)
- Suggests thematic strengthening ("Betrayal appears in 9 scenes—consider making it central motif?")
- Dashboard widget: "Top 5 emerging motifs this week"

### **Gap & Missing Element Detector**

- Flags structural/story gaps:
  - "No introduction scene for Kofi—add in Act I?"
  - "Subplot C has only 1 scene—consider expanding?"
  - "Act III lacks a clear climax beat."
- Suggests placement: faint drop zones or "Recommended here?" indicators

### **Module-Specific AI Editorial Integrations**

### **Manuscript-Specific AI Features**

| **Feature**                          | **Description**                                                                                 |
| ------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Inline Linking Intelligence**      | @-mention detection → suggests Wiki links; flags spelling inconsistencies                       |
| **Meta-Panel Auto-Fill & Flags**     | Populates presence lists; warns on missing POV/location/tone                                    |
| **Continuity Check During Drafting** | Subtle sidebar badges: "Scene mentions 'the Whisperer' but not introduced earlier."             |
| **Scene Intent Reminder**            | Shows scene goal from Meta-Panel ("Advance romance + plant clue") while writing                 |
| **Snapshot Intelligence**            | After large edit: "Big change detected—want to snapshot first?"                                 |
| **Emotional Tone Consistency**       | Suggests tone tag if text sentiment drifts ("This scene feels tense—update mood to 'anxious'?") |

### **User Control & Customization**

| **Control**                 | **Description**                                                                           |
| --------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Assistance Level Slider** | Minimal → Balanced → Deep; controls frequency and depth of suggestions                    |
| **Module-Specific Toggles** | Enable/disable AI in Canvas only, Manuscript only, etc.                                   |
| **Snooze & Ignore**         | Right-click any flag/suggestion → "Ignore this type", "Snooze 7 days", "Never show again" |
| **Feedback Loop**           | Thumbs up/down on suggestions → improves future accuracy (stored anonymously per user)    |

## **16. Technical Specifications**

### **Stack Alignment**

- Built on OdinPad stack: React/Next.js + Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, Storage, Realtime)
- Rich text editor with TipTap foundation (collaborative-ready)
- PWA for offline writing with IndexedDB sync
- WebSocket for real-time collaboration (Phase 2)
- Focus Mode uses CSS containment + requestAnimationFrame for buttery-smooth typewriter scroll
- Ambient audio via Web Audio API (low-latency, volume-normalized loops)

### **Editor Performance**

- **Large Document Support:** Handles 200,000+ words smoothly
- **Virtual Scrolling:** Only visible content rendered
- **Debounced Auto-Save:** Every 3 seconds
- **Offline Queue:** Changes cached locally, sync when online

### **Data Model**

| **Entity**             | **Key Fields**                                                                                                                                                                   |
| ---------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Scene**              | id, project_id, binder_item_id, title, content (JSON), synopsis, status, pov, location, timeline_date, emotional_tone, thread_tags, word_count, created_at, updated_at           |
| **Revision**           | id, scene_id, snapshot_id, content_snapshot, created_at, is_auto_save, is_manual_save, label, change_summary                                                                     |
| **ManuscriptSettings** | id, user_id, theme, font, font_size, line_height, margins, typewriter_enabled, ambient_sound, typewriter_scroll_behavior, vignette_intensity                                     |
| **Bookmark**           | id, user_id, scene_id, label, created_at                                                                                                                                         |
| **MetaPanel**          | id, scene_id, synopsis, pov, location, emotional_tone, plot_threads, arcs_impacted, timeline_position, setup_payoff_notes, foreshadowing_checklist, revision_notes, scene_intent |
| **Gamification**       | id, user_id, points, rank, badges_earned, streak_count, last_active                                                                                                              |

### **Performance Considerations**

- Debounced auto-save (every 3 seconds) to reduce database writes
- Content stored as JSON for rich text flexibility
- Revision snapshots stored as efficient diffs (delta compression) + full-text preview cache
- Offline-first with IndexedDB; sync on reconnect
- Snapshot Drawer built with Radix UI + smooth spring animations
